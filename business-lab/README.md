# Business Lab — Pepino Pick Side Business

## Goal

Fully automated business, $3000+/month, max 2 hours/day human time.
Operated by Roman + wife. Powered by AI agents.

## Process

1. Research → 15 business ideas analyzed
2. Filter → 3 hypotheses selected
3. Prototype → MVP for each hypothesis (1 week each)
4. Validate → Real market test
5. Launch → 1 winner goes live

## Constraints

- Startup budget: $500
- Location: Córdoba, Argentina
- Languages: RU, ES, EN
- Infrastructure: VPS, Claude Code, OpenClaw, n8n, Docker
- Time: 2 hours/day max (Roman) + 1-2 hours (wife)
- Separate from Pepino Pick (but can use its tools/skills)

## Structure

- research/ — market research, competitor analysis
- hypotheses/ — 3 selected business hypotheses
- prototypes/ — MVP code and configs
- tools/ — shared utilities
