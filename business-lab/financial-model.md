- Pricing: Starter $15K ARS/mo, Pro $30K, Enterprise $60K
- Revenue in USD (Blue rate 1425): ~$10, $21, $42/mo per client
- Target: 15 clients avg $25/mo = $375/mo by month 6

### Stream 2: AI Automation Agency

- Pricing: Projects $500-5000, Retainers $100-1000/mo
- Target: 3 retainer clients avg $400/mo = $1,200/mo by month 6

### Stream 3: Pepino Academy (Courses)

- Pricing: Mini $49, Full $199, Workshop $150/person
- Target: 15 mini + 5 full + 2 workshops = $2,630/mo by month 6

## Decision Criteria: When to Double Down

### RestoBot -> Scale if:

- 5+ paying clients by month 3
- NPS > 8
- Churn < 10%
- Action: hire part-time salesperson

### Agency -> Scale if:

- 3+ retainer clients by month 3
- Avg project > $1,500
- Repeat rate > 50%
- Action: raise prices, specialize in niche

### Academy -> Scale if:

- 50+ course sales by month 3
- YouTube > 2,000 subs
- Workshop waitlist exists
- Action: launch full course, raise workshop price

### Kill criteria (stop if by month 4):

- RestoBot: 0 paying clients after free trials
- Agency: <$500/mo after 3 months effort
- Academy: <10 course sales total
