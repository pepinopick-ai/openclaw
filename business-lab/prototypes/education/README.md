- Languages: Russian, Spanish, English
- Content:
  - Day 1: What is an AI Agent OS (concept + architecture)

  - Day 3: Your first automation script (CJS/Node.js)

- Format: 12 modules, video + text + templates
- Languages: Russian first, then Spanish
- Content:

  - Module 4-6: Business logic (CRM, inventory, pricing)
  - Module 7-9: AI layer (Claude integration, demand forecast, alerts)
  - Module 10-11: Operations (monitoring, self-healing, pipelines)
  - Module 12: Scale (multi-tenant, SaaS packaging)

- Target: 10 sales/month = $1,990/month

- Format: Live Zoom, 6 people max, hands-on
