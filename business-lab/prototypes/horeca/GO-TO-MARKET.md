# RestoAI — Go-to-Market Plan

## Week 1: Foundation

- [ ] Finish horeca-engine.cjs (multi-tenant core)
- [ ] Deploy landing page (Vercel/Cloudflare Pages)
- [ ] Set up WhatsApp Business for leads
- [ ] Create 3-min demo video (screen recording)
- [ ] Prepare 1-page PDF proposal in Spanish

## Week 2: First 3 Pilots (FREE)

- [ ] Offer FREE 30-day pilot to 3 best restaurant clients
  - Target: restaurants that buy most from Pepino Pick
  - Pitch: "Estamos probando una herramienta de IA para restaurantes. ¿Querés ser beta tester gratis?"
- [ ] Set up Google Sheets for each pilot
- [ ] Configure Telegram/WhatsApp bot per restaurant
- [ ] Daily check-ins for first week

## Week 3-4: Validate

- [ ] Collect feedback from 3 pilots
- [ ] Track: which modules they use most, what's confusing
- [ ] Calculate: how much waste they actually reduced
- [ ] Get testimonials (text or video)

## Month 2: First Paying Customers

- [ ] Convert pilots to paid ($15K-30K ARS/mes)
- [ ] Referral program: "Recomendá a otro restaurante, recibí 1 mes gratis"
- [ ] Approach 5-10 more restaurants via WhatsApp
- [ ] Local restaurant association (Cámara de Gastronómicos de Córdoba)

## Month 3-4: Scale to 10-15

- [ ] Instagram page @restoai.ar
- [ ] Case study: "Restaurant X reduced waste 25% in 30 days"
- [ ] Google My Business listing
- [ ] Partner with food suppliers (cross-referrals)

## Pricing Strategy (ARS)

| Plan       | ARS/mes | USD equiv | Target          |
| ---------- | ------- | --------- | --------------- |
| Starter    | 15.000  | ~$10      | Small cafes     |
| Pro        | 30.000  | ~$21      | Mid restaurants |
| Enterprise | 60.000  | ~$42      | Multi-location  |

Note: Price in ARS, charge via MercadoPago recurring.
For international clients: charge in USD via Stripe.

## Risk Mitigation

- Restaurants don't pay → extend free trial, prove ROI first
- Too much support → create self-service onboarding + FAQ bot
- Competition copies → first-mover advantage + deeper AI features
- ARS devaluation → switch to USD pricing for larger clients
